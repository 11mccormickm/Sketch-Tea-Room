tea 101
  • black tea
  • The world's best black teas are whole leaf and produced in India, Sri Lanka and China. Assertive and robust, they can be enjoyed with or without milk.
  • green tea
  • Green tea is the oldest type of tea in the world. Great green teas are characterized by vibrant spring freshness coupled with a quenching, sweet finish.
  • oolong tea
  • Oolong teas are a speciality of Southeast China and Taiwan. Their production is complex and involves great skill. They offer a profusion of flavours from light notes of fresh flowers to richly textured roasted hazels and chocolate.
  • white tea
  • White tea originates in the spectacular mountains of Fuding in China. It is the least processed of all teas, beautifully delicate with notes of honey and melon.
